What does the data tell us?

The most recent data available explain to us the categorical differences of the income of every country in the world. The droughtful income in for example Africa is visualized by the desert yellow color, while western countries with high GDP per capita show their higher welfare with saturated, dark colors of red. The scatter plot visible beneath the world map displays the history of the annual growth as default for the whole world, but clicking a country on the map will load the scatter of the historic economical growth for that country.
